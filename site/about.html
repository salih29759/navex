About

Operators first, advisors second

Navex Capital serves private equity investors, capital providers, and business owners in the maritime, shipping, transport, and logistics sectors, combining strategic insight with hands-on operating leadership. We focus on sub-$200M deals in the US, Canada, and Europe.

Who we are

A specialist boutique, built by people who have run the business

Most advisors in our space come from banking or consulting. We come from the bridge and the boardroom. The founding team has spent decades operating and leading maritime and transport companies through growth, integration, and exit, and that perspective shapes every engagement.

We work where financial capital is plentiful but focused operational expertise is not, helping investors and owners make faster, higher-quality decisions and reach better outcomes.

Mission

To give private equity firms, institutional investors, and business owners the strategic insight, hands-on leadership, and diligence rigor that drive durable growth and lasting enterprise value across maritime, transport, logistics, and marine services.

Vision

To be the strategic partner and board-level advisor of choice for capital allocators and operators navigating investment, transformation, and growth across the global maritime and logistics landscape.

Why Navex

We advise on the deal, then help run the business after

Bankers broker the transaction and move on. We do the commercial and operational work before you commit, then step in to run the value-creation after you close. One team across the full lifecycle.
