Privacy

We collect very little, and only to respond to you. This page explains what and why.

Last updated: 2026

What we collect

If you use our contact form or email us, we receive the details you choose to provide: your name, company, job title, email address, and message. We do not sell or rent this information, and we do not use it for advertising.

How we use it

We use what you send only to respond to your enquiry and, where relevant, to discuss a potential engagement. Communications are treated as confidential.

Cookies and analytics

This site is intentionally lightweight. It does not set marketing cookies. If we add basic, privacy-respecting analytics in future, we will update this page.

Data retention

We keep enquiry correspondence only as long as needed for the conversation it relates to, or as required by law, and then delete it.

Your rights

You can ask us what information we hold about you, or ask us to correct or delete it, at any time. Contact us and we will act on reasonable requests promptly.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to info@navexcapital.com.
