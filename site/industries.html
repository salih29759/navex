Industries

Sectors we know from the inside.

Deep, hands-on experience across the maritime value chain: the businesses our founding team has operated and led, not just analyzed.

Maritime & Shipping

Owners, operators, and fleets across dry bulk, tankers, container, and specialized tonnage. We understand fleet economics, asset cycles, and charter strategy, and how vessel values move, so an investment case is built on how the sector actually behaves rather than a generic model.

Marine Services

Towage, offshore support, subsea, survey, and the specialist vessel businesses that keep ports and energy infrastructure running. These are people-and-asset businesses where utilization, contract quality, and crewing decide the margin, and where real operating experience is hard to fake.

Port & Terminal Logistics

Terminals, stevedoring, handling, and storage. We focus on throughput, berth and yard productivity, capex discipline, and the commercial terms that determine returns, with a clear view of where automation pays and where it does not.

Transport & Logistics

Seaborne freight, intermodal connections, and the supply-chain businesses that link them. We assess network economics, contract quality, and the operational levers that protect margin through the cycle.

Why it matters

These sectors get mispriced by people who have never run them.

Capital-intensive, operationally complex maritime businesses do not behave like a spreadsheet. Asset cycles, charter exposure, crewing, and utilization swing returns in ways generic diligence misses. Our founding team has operated inside these businesses, so we price the risk others overlook and find the value others walk past.
